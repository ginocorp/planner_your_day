"Planner" Your Day

Daily Planner App to keep you on schedule from start of your day to the end

How it works!
  • Click on any corresponding text area,
    type your plan/goal for the hour,
    then click the save button!

  • grey text area:
    the hour has already passed

  • yellow text area:
    the hour you are currently on

  • green text area:
    the hour has not passed yet
