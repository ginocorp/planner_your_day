"Planner" Your Day

Daily Planner App to keep you on schedule from the start of your day to the end of it!

How does it work?
  • Click on any corresponding text area,
    type your plan/goal for the hour,
    then click the save button!

  • grey text area:
    this hour has passed

  • yellow text area:
    this is the current hour

  • green text area:
    this hour is in the future
